Will Bitcoin hit $100k this year? Will Ethereum double? These questions have prices on prediction markets every day.

## Three Key Features of Crypto Prediction Markets

1. **24/7 continuous trading**: Unlike stock markets, crypto markets never close. Prices reflect real-time global consensus.
2. **High-stakes participation**: Real money on the line creates strong incentives.
3. **Extreme volatility**: A single news event can shift probabilities in minutes.

## Reading Crypto Prediction Contracts

A contract for "Bitcoin exceeds $150k by end of 2026" trading at $0.42 on Polymarket means the market assigns a 42% probability.

## Why Prediction Markets Work for Crypto

Crypto is the optimal testing ground for prediction markets because:
- No trading halts or limits; prices can fully reflect information
- Global liquidity and diverse participants
- Clear settlement; no gray areas
